Devlog for the videogame I've been making since April 2022, titled Amethyst Dawn.

Entry 0: April 2022

Entry "zero" is accounting for all work done on the game pre-APW.

Amethyst Dawn is a 2D story-based dungeon crawler in which you descend (or ascend) into dungeons, connected by towns and hub areas. It takes place in a fantasy world. The spread of ravenous, vicious monsters has driven the humans into tight settlements, where it's easier to defend, and thus safer. The reason the were so easily overtaken is due to the strange nature of these monsters: their complete immunity toconventional weapons. All attempts to hack and slash at them have failed, and it seems the only way to damage them is through the use of spectres. Spectres are spirits of magic, little orb-shaped beings that are bound to the creature that summoned them. In order to be able to summon spectres, one must collect monster viscera and have a current Drifter perform the ritual. Because of the constant moving these fighters have to do to protect people, they coined the name "Drifters" - which stuck. Drifters have become the first (and in many cases, only) line of defense against monsters. Drifter's guilds form, for the sole purpose of defending and attempting to reclaim the above ground territory occupied by monsters.
